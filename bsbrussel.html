Have you ever wanted to make you veggies more fun? This brown sugar brussel sprout recipe is sure to impress!

Ingredients

  • Brussel Sprounds
  • 2 Tabelspoons Brown Sugar
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2 tabelspoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 clove Garlic
  • 1/2 stick of Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450
  2. Remove the stems from the sprouts and then cut them in half
  3. Toss the sprouts in a bowl with olive oil, salt and pepper
  4. Place the sprouts in the oven for 20 minutes
  5. Pre-heat a pan on the stove under medium-low heat
  6. Half way through the sprouts cook time melt the butter in a pan, then add the brownsugar, garlic and soysauce into the pan
  7. After the sprouts finish cooking toss them in the pan to combine
  8. Plate and enjoy!
